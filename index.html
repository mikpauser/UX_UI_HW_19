EXPERTISE

User Research & User Empathy

This is one of my favorite parts of the design process because it’s truly the basis for creating meaningful and memorable design. I like to analyze both qualitative and quantitative data to truly understand our users’ habits, patterns and behaviors.

Human-Centered Visual Design

Now that we know our users, the fun can begin. Visual design is what drew me to study UX/UI. I love working on the look and feel of a digital product. I focus on creating aesthetically pleasing graphic material by using dynamic color palettes, typography, and layouts in an eye-catching and innovative way.

Prototyping & Front-End Dev

Bringing the design to life is truly the most rewarding part of any project. I love each step of the designs iteration. Most of all, the final iteration process, utilizing data and insights gained from usability tests to further elevate the product. I also enjoy seeing how far the project has come and the rapid iteration process we, as designers, go through to get there.
